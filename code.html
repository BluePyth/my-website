Gatling is a new stress tool. Coded in Scala, it uses Akka and Netty to achieve its great efficiency. I've been one of the two initial committers of the project. Now I have less time to contribute, but I try to write about it and spread the word...

VuzZz is an Android Application that was created by a group of four in 48 hours during February 2012's Google Android DevCamp — organized my the PAUG (Paris Android User Group). It gathers information about a place of your choice and gives a mark according to various criteria. It uses Paris and Nantes Open Data.
